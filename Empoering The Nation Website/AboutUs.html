About Empowering The Nation

Our Mission and Background

Empowering the Nation is a Johannesburg-based small enterprise founded by Precious Radebe in 2022.

Our core mission is to focus on skills development and training for domestic workers and gardeners, helping them to become more employable, earn higher incomes, and build sustainable small businesses.
