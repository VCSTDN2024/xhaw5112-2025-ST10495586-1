Welcome

Empowering the Nation is a Johannesburg-based small enterprise, founded by Precious Radebe in 2022. Our focus is on skills development and training to help our learners become more employable, earn higher incomes, and build sustainable small businesses.

Our proposed web platform and mobile app will allow users to advertise training courses, access detailed course information, calculate course fees (including discounts and VAT), and request bookings or contact us directly.
